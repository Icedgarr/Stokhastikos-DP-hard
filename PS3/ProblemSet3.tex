First we will show that $Q_p^* = \sqrt{n}Q^* + \mu(n- \sqrt{n})$%
\begin{align*}
	P (\sum_{L= 1}^n D_i \leq Q_p^*) = &  \frac{b}{b+R} \\
	= & P(\sqrt{n}D_i + \mu(n - \sqrt{n})\leq Q_p^*) = \frac{b}{b+R} \\
	= & P(D_i \leq \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}(Q_p^*- \mu(n-\sqrt{n})))= \frac{b}{b + h}
\end{align*}
Thus, 
\begin{align*}
